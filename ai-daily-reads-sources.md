# AI Daily Reads - source list

This file lists the resources the AI agent reviews each evening when curating the
"AI Daily Reads" page. It is a living list: Hossein can add, remove, or reprioritize
entries at any time, and the agent should read this file at the start of every run and
treat it as the primary set of places to check (while staying free to follow strong
leads beyond it).

How to use this file:
- The agent should scan these sources for anything published in the past ~24 hours.
- It then selects only the genuine must-reads (3-6 ceiling, fewer on quiet days).
- New sources can be added under the relevant section below. Keep one entry per line
  in the format: `Name - URL - short note on what it is good for`.

---

## News and analysis (general AI landscape)
- The Decoder - https://the-decoder.com/ - timely, well-summarized AI news across research, products, policy.
- Build Fast with AI (daily roundup) - https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/collection/ai-industry-news-trends - long daily "biggest stories" digests, good for cross-checking.
- Hacker News (front page) - https://news.ycombinator.com/ - what builders are actually discussing today.
- TechCrunch AI - https://techcrunch.com/category/artificial-intelligence/ - industry, funding, product launches.
- The Verge AI - https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence - consumer-facing AI news and context.
- Ars Technica AI - https://arstechnica.com/ai/ - deeper technical and policy coverage.
- MIT Technology Review (AI) - https://www.technologyreview.com/topic/artificial-intelligence/ - thoughtful analysis pieces.

## Research papers
- Hugging Face Daily Papers - https://huggingface.co/papers - community-voted trending papers, with plain summaries.
- arXiv cs.AI (recent) - https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/recent - raw firehose of new AI papers.
- arXiv cs.CL (recent) - https://arxiv.org/list/cs.CL/recent - natural language processing / LLMs.
- arXiv cs.LG (recent) - https://arxiv.org/list/cs.LG/recent - machine learning broadly.
- Papers with Code (trending) - https://paperswithcode.com/ - papers tied to code and benchmarks.

## Labs, companies, and official announcements
- OpenAI news - https://openai.com/news/ - first-party releases and changelogs.
- Google DeepMind blog - https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/ - research and model releases.
- Anthropic news - https://www.anthropic.com/news - model and policy announcements.
- Meta AI blog - https://ai.meta.com/blog/ - open models and research.
- Mistral AI news - https://mistral.ai/news/ - European frontier-model updates.
- Hugging Face blog - https://huggingface.co/blog - open-source model and tooling launches.

## Community and discussion (notable takes, "tweets, etc.")
- r/MachineLearning - https://www.reddit.com/r/MachineLearning/ - researcher discussion and paper threads.
- r/LocalLLaMA - https://www.reddit.com/r/LocalLLaMA/ - open-model and on-device community pulse.
- Simon Willison's blog - https://simonwillison.net/ - sharp, practical write-ups on new AI tooling.
- Import AI (Jack Clark) - https://importai.substack.com/ - weekly synthesis of what mattered.

## Policy, safety, and economics
- Stanford HAI - https://hai.stanford.edu/news - policy, economics, and societal impact.
- AI policy / government sources (e.g. FERC, EU AI Act pages) - checked when a specific story points to them.

---

Notes for the agent:
- Prefer primary sources for the link in each item when possible (the lab's own post,
  the arXiv abstract page, the official filing), even if a news site surfaced it first.
- Cover the whole landscape: research, big-lab releases, tooling/agents, industry and
  business, policy, and economics. Do not over-index on a single category in one day.
- Always verify a link resolves before publishing it.
